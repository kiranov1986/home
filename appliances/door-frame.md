# Master Bedroom Door Frame

A modern PVC door frame securing the master bedroom entrance. This frame is built to last with professional-grade adhesive.

## Installation Details

**Location:** Master Bedroom  
**Material:** PVC (durable and low-maintenance)  
**Adhesive:** Pidilite Fevibond (professional-grade bonding)  

*PVC frames are weather-resistant and require minimal maintenance - just occasional cleaning to keep them looking fresh!*
